Important Information

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic situation school hours will be altered. All the grades will be divided into two group, each group including 15-17 students. The first group will start its lessons at 8:00 with reduced hours and it will finish at 11:10. The second group will start its lessons at 11:30 until 14:40. It is very important for all the students and faculty members to follow the instructions to protect themselves and others alike. Please read our full instructions and rules.
