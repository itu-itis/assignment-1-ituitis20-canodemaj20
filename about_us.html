Our History

On October 20, 1947, when Albania had just been liberated, the first general high school in history opened its doors in Vlora. of this city. "Ali Demi" high school of Vlora, one of the most important high schools from the '50s until today which is considered a cultural monument of national education. The high school was opened in a two-storey house near the old church Catholic.In 1969 he finally settled in the building that is still in the city center today. The first director it was Koço Papaproko who taught geography and history. In the history of "Ali Demi" high school, the names of the directors Koço Papaproko, Harilla Kuçuli, Ali Belshi, Eneida Vera et al. And Roland Dervishaj (current director) who, although with very different but virtuous features, for more than seven decades have given a dignified direction to the teaching and educational work of generations, This collective of excellent pedagogy has turned the school into the most beloved temple of knowledge that Vlora has had. Boundless worship and gratitude today is dedicated to the excellent teachers who laid the first stone of teaching in the high school "Ali Demi", such as "Teachers of the People" or those pedagogues who became the symbol of eternal of this enlightened school, as the "Deserved Teachers." Students from all over the city of Vlora were enrolled in the “Ali Demi” high school. From 1950 to 1990 they were educated in it a total of 3388 students. The extraterrestrial world of alidemas was filled with a long series of activities. in '60s and '70s they received a series of first prizes in the theater high school olympiads of Albania and published then some almanacs with literary creations. The pedagogical staff of the high school "Ali Demi" Sava in December 1990 received the credit historical that was among the rare collectives in Albania that with a special message welcomed the arrival of pluralism political in our country.In addition to the glorious history of the high school, there is also a large number of former students of the high school "Ali Demi", who became excellent students, who were and are prominent figures of the academic world in Albania and in the world, such as: Bardhyl Golemi, Emin Lafe, Ethem Lika, Aurela Pasku, Agim Muço, Vesel Peçuli, Arta Balili, etc. But this epochal education center in Vlora should have its most natural pride for that group of former students of who later became politicians and gave and give a valuable contribution to the entire state system of Albania, such as: Abedin Elmazi, Luiza Hoxha, Koço Kokëdhima etc. Equally honored should be felt nowadays Vlora public opinion with those talented former students of the high school "Ali Demi ”, who excelled as personalities with their achievements in the history of Albanian sports, such as: Mexhit Haxhiu, Aferdita Aliaj, Kastriot Malaj, Ilir Kallfa etc. This high school has been a "combine" of winners of national olympiads as well as international competitions of new talents in all fields of science.
