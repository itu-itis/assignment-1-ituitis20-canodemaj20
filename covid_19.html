Covid-19 Important Information

Rules and instructions that must be followed

  • Students' temperatures must be measured when they enter and leave the building with a digital thermometer
  • Students' hands must be sprayed with a sort of alcohol or other disinfector
  • All students and all faculty members must wear a mask at all times
  • Students tables must be at least 1.5 metres away from each other
  • In the classroom the windows must remain open to ensure that clean air enters the room
  • School activities must not include groups with more than 10 students and always keeping their social distance
  • Students must not leave their classroom or stand in the hallways
  • If a student has the need to use the bathroom he/she will be accompanied the person who is in charge
  • Students must pay close attention to their personal hygiene
  • After the lesson has finished all classrooms must be disinfected by the responsible school emplyees
  • Students should inform the school if they had contact with a positive case of Covid-19
  • If a student has symptoms of Covid-19, he/she must contact the school and should not attend the lesson until further notice
